More about Kanna?

Kanna Kamui (カンナカムイ) also known as Kanna Kobayashi (小林 カンナ Kobayashi Kanna), is one of the main characters in Kobayashi-san Chi no Maid Dragon and the main protagonist of Kobayashi-san Chi no Maid Dragon: Kanna no Nichijou. Kanna is a young female dragon, who is exiled from her world as a consequence of her pranks.

Commands

Prefix

k! prefix is always lowercase

Commands
  • kannasay (your message) Let's Kanna hold a sign with your custom text.
  • hug (@user) Hug's the mentioned user.
  • kiss (@user) Kisses the mentioned user.
  • punch (@user) Punches the mentioned user.
  • poke (@user) Pokes the mentioned user.
  • slap (@user) Slaps the mentioned user.
  • supreme (your message) Creates a image with your text in the supreme font.
